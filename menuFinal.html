Welcome to the Wonderful Non-Wizarding World of Christopher Rescorla!

Greetings! My name is Christopher Rescorla and I will be your host for the next however long you are here. In this venue, a few questions will be answered and perhaps more will be generated. Am I really a mystery, wrapped in an enigma, wrapped in bacon? Probably not. It would take a long time to wrap me up in bacon. But that is neither here nor there.

What you'll really find here is some of the things that make me me. It's somewhat a place to brag about myself and a place to relate some of the things that I enjoy. But perhaps more importantly, it's a place for me to explore just what I can do with developing a web site and trying to push myself further than I could have even imagined a year ago. It's also a place to display my projects both in web development and with 3D graphics and modelling, two things I am quite passionate about even if there are plenty of times in both were they just make my brain hurt...
